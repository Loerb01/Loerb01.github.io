Q: Why does this website look like this?

A: Mostly because I'm lazy

For a longer answer I would reccomend starting with Bjarne Stroustrup, motherfuckingwebsite, and bettermotherfuckingwebsite. To be a bit more specific, I'm not a web dev/designer, or anything close to that, other than something casual like this. In fact, I actually dislike and take issue with many modern sites. Sure many of them look quite nice, but scrolljacking, incessant cookie notifications, and generally busy desings unnecessarily take up computer power, brain power, and my patience. It's probably a bit overcompensatory, but I refuse to contribute to the problem. I have come to believe that the internet is actively becoming more hostile to users both purposefully and more often due to design that is ignorant of resources that tend to have limits such as bandwith and personal computer power. All that being said, I still include a small bit of CSS for personal preference reasons (mostly to use a sans-serif font, which is a rant for another time), but if you aren't a fan change it, I encourage you. If you particularly like your changes, feel free to send them to me. All that being said, please don't let it distract you from that original answer. I'm mostly too lazy to do more, but I'm ok with that because I don't think it is, or should be, necessary. P.S. Even if I do style this site more one day, I still plan on avoiding Javascript like the plague. It is a mistake to use, especially with things as wide spread as webpages.
